ABOUT ME

Hi guys! My name is Jawad Saad and I am a full stack developer. I've always loved computers and for as long as I can remember, I've been the go-to guy in my family for sorting out everyone's tech issues. My first ever experience of web design came at age 13 using Microsoft Frontpage during Computing class in school (if you've never heard of Frontpage, I wouldn't be surprised). I enjoyed building websites but never pursued it until very recently when I decided I needed a career change.

I had been working as a manager at a mobility store in Fulham, West London, but had grown tired of it as there was no more room for career progression there. I must say, however, that I owe that place a lot as not only was it my first job, it was also the job that inspired me to pursue web design and software development as I had taken it upon myself while I was there to build a website (using a template from 123-reg) for the business. After building it, it was down to me to maintain and update it. I came to realise that this was the most enjoyable part of my job so I decided to pursue it further.

I joined Codecademy and took some online courses in building websites from scratch as I still had no idea how to do this yet. Mid-way through this course, it hit me that I should join some sort of coding bootcamp. After some Googling around, I found Just IT. I'm glad to say that I am currently on a self-funded course with Just IT called the Developers Professional Programme. This has really helped me build up my skillset and I now feel confident that I can have a successful career in this field.
